How the Program Works

Step by step
1
Before You Start
Complete Your Intake Form
Fill out your medical history, vitals, and contraindication screening. Upload baseline labs. Your physician reviews everything within 24–48 hours.
2
Week 1–4
Start at 2.5mg Weekly
Begin with the lowest dose to allow your body to adjust. Weekly self-injection using your physician-prepared guide. Monitor weight, appetite, and any side effects.
3
Week 5 — Reassessment
Physician Check-In Required
Message MedGlow Wellness before changing your dose. Your physician will assess your weight loss, tolerance, and side effects before clearing you to escalate to 5mg.
4
Week 5 Onwards
Gradual Dose Escalation
If cleared, dose increases every 4 weeks (5mg → 7.5mg → 10mg) based on your response. Every escalation requires physician approval.
5
Every 4 Weeks
Monthly Monitoring
Send your weight, blood pressure, heart rate, and side effect notes. Labs repeated every 3–6 months. Dose adjusted as needed.
✦
Throughout the Program
Always Available
Message MedGlow Wellness anytime via WhatsApp or Messenger for questions, concerns, or any unwanted events. You are never alone in this program. 🌿
