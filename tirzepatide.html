🔬 Baseline Laboratory Requirements

Before starting the Tirzepatide program, the following laboratory tests are required to assess your overall health status and ensure the program is safe for you. Labs will also be repeated every 3–6 months to monitor your progress.

CBC with Platelet
FBS (Fasting Blood Sugar)
SGPT / SGOT
Thyroid Panel
Serum Creatinine
